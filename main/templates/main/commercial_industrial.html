Commercial Structures

There are several advantages to using pre-engineered steel buildings in the construction of commercial buildings:

  • Speed of Construction: The pre-engineered steel building system is a modular system that is fabricated in a controlled environment. This allows for the construction of the building to be completed in a fraction of the time it would take to build a conventional building, allowing you to get down to business and start making money as quickly as possible.
  • Flexibility: The pre-engineered steel building system is a modular system that can be designed to fit your specific needs. The building can be designed to be as large or as small as you need, and can be designed to fit your specific needs. Pre-engineered steel buildings can be easily modified or expanded as the needs of the business change over time.
  • Cost of Construction: Pre-engineered steel buildings are typically less expensive than traditional building methods, as they require less labour and materials.
  • Durability: Steel is a strong and durable material that can withstand harsh weather conditions and heavy loads. Pre-engineered steel buildings are designed to last for many years with minimal maintenance.
  • Energy Efficiency: Pre-engineered steel buildings can be designed with energy-efficient features such as insulation, vapour barrier, and energy-efficient windows to reduce energy consumption.
  • Low Maintenance: Pre-engineered steel buildings are designed to be low maintenance. The steel frame is designed to be rust resistant, and the exterior cladding is designed to be low maintenance.
  • Fire Resistance: The steel frame, exterior cladding, and insulation are all designed to be fire-resistant.
  • Sustainability: Steel is a sustainable building material that can be recycled and reused, making pre-engineered steel buildings an environmentally-friendly choice.
  • Aesthetics: Pre-engineered steel buildings can be designed to have a modern and attractive appearance, making them suitable for a variety of commercial applications.

Whether your needs are a restaurant, grocery store, lumber supply, shopping center or car dealership, we provide you with a steel building system to fit your needs and budget. By working closely with us, you can plan on a project that is lower in cost and faster to build than most conventional buildings. Owners, architects, engineers, and developers choose steel buildings for their beauty, functional design, short completion time, and durability.

Steel buildings are also the most environmentally friendly building system available. They are 100% recyclable and can be designed to meet LEED requirements.

  • Processing Plants

  • Trucking Terminals

These are just a few examples of the types of industrial buildings that can be constructed using pre-engineered steel buildings. The versatility of steel as a building material means that it can be used to create structures of almost any size, shape, or function.
