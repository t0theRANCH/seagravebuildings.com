About Us

Seagrave Building Systems Ltd. is a Canadian-owned company specializing in supplying of steel buildings systems. Since 1982, Seagrave has supplied steel buildings for a wide variety of applications including industrial, commercial, recreational, institutional and farm related structures.

Our materials are “steel” based for strength with state-of-the-art technology in providing a super strong, maintenance-free, stylish building. Our superior engineering can meet or exceed all building code strength requirements in your area for your long-term protection. Whether it is snow blizzards or hurricane force winds, Seagrave Building Systems has stood up to the test time and time again!
